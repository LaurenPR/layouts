Dataset

PWD Monitoring Locations

Description

Locations where PWD has conducted surface water quality sampling and other types of stream assessments. Monitoring activities may include water quality grab sampling, habitat assessment, and sampling of invertebrates, fish and algae from wadeable streams. Not all assessment activities are performed at all sites.

Development Process

Point feature class developed for linking and visualizing on site assessment data.

Thematic Mapping

SITE_ID is a combination of a watershed prefix (e.g., WS = Wissahcikon Creek) and the rivermile distance from the confluence with the next water body in hundredths of a mile. WS1850 = Wissahickon Creek 18.5 miles from the confluence with the Schuylkill River.
